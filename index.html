About Shawn Christie

Local, Experienced, and Dedicated to Quality

Shawn Christie started his company in 2004, building on years of experience in general construction to deliver top-quality painting and exterior services across Sarasota and Bradenton.

With over two decades of local service, Shawn knows Florida homes — the climate challenges, the materials that last, and the craftsmanship that homeowners deserve. He lives in the community he serves and takes personal pride in every project.
