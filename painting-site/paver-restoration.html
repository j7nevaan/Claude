Paver Restoration

Restore Your Pavers to Like-New Condition

Florida's heat, rain, and algae growth can quickly turn beautiful pavers into dull, stained, and faded surfaces. Our paver restoration process doesn't just clean the surface — we restore color, re-sand the joints, and apply a professional sealer that protects your investment for years to come.

Whether your pavers are brick, travertine, concrete, or natural stone, Shawn Christie has the experience and equipment to bring them back to life.

  • High-pressure deep cleaning
  • Efflorescence and stain removal
  • Polymeric sand re-jointing
  • Color-enhancing or natural-look sealers
  • Weed and moss prevention
  • Long-lasting protection from Florida weather

The Process

Our 4-Step Paver Restoration Process

1

Deep Clean

High-pressure washing removes all dirt, algae, oil, and stains from every paver surface.

2

Re-Sand Joints

Polymeric sand is applied to fill joints, stabilize pavers, and prevent weed growth.

3

Apply Sealer

Professional-grade sealer is applied to protect against UV, rain, and staining.

4

Final Inspection

We walk the area with you to ensure every square foot looks perfect.
