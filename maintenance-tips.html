Home Maintenance Tips

Keep your Sarasota home looking its best year-round with these expert maintenance tips.

Expert Advice

Florida Home Maintenance Checklist

🌞

Annual Pressure Washing

Have your driveway, walkways, and exterior walls pressure washed at least once a year to prevent biological buildup.

🖌

Paint Every 5-7 Years

Florida's sun degrades exterior paint faster than in northern states. Inspect and repaint exterior surfaces every 5-7 years.

🧱

Reseal Pavers Every 2-3 Years

Paver sealers wear down over time. Check annually and reseal every 2-3 years to maintain protection and appearance.

📌

Soft-Wash Roof Annually

Black streaks and algae on roofs are common in Florida. Annual soft-wash cleaning protects your roof and maintains appearance.

🌘

Caulk Inspection

Florida's temperature swings cause caulk to crack. Inspect and replace caulk around windows, doors, and trim yearly.

🧳

Grout Maintenance

Tile grout should be sealed periodically and cleaned regularly to prevent staining and mildew growth, especially in wet areas.
