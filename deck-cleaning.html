Deck Cleaning & Staining

Restore your deck to its former glory. Cleaning, staining, and painting for wood and composite decks.

Deck Services

Bring Your Deck Back to Life

Florida's sun, rain, and humidity are tough on decks. Algae, mold, gray weathering, and UV bleaching can make even a solid deck look old and neglected. Our deck cleaning and staining process removes all of that and adds a protective coat that keeps your deck looking great season after season.

  • High-pressure cleaning to remove mold & grime
  • Wood brightener application
  • Any needed board replacements
  • Premium deck stain or paint
  • Solid, semi-transparent, or clear finishes
