Exterior Painting

Florida-Tough Exterior Finishes

Florida's intense sun, humidity, and afternoon storms are brutal on exterior paint. A proper exterior paint job isn't just about looks — it's your home's first line of defense against moisture intrusion, UV damage, and wood rot.

Shawn Christie has been painting Sarasota and Bradenton exteriors since 2004. We know which products stand up to Florida's climate, how to properly prep stucco, wood, and Hardie board, and how to deliver a finish that looks great for years.

  • Pressure wash before painting
  • All cracks and gaps caulked and filled
  • Primer applied to bare surfaces
  • 100% acrylic, weather-resistant paints
  • Proper coating on stucco, wood & more
  • Cleanup of all debris and drop cloths
