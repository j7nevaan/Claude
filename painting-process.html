Our Painting Process

From your first call to the final touch-up, here's exactly how we deliver perfect results every time.

How It Works

The Christie Painting Process

1

Free Estimate

We visit your property, assess the scope, and provide a detailed written estimate — no surprises.

2

Color Consultation

Not sure on color? We help you choose the right shades for your home's style and surroundings.

3

Prep & Protect

We move furniture, lay drop cloths, mask trim, and fill holes and cracks before a single drop of paint goes down.

4

Prime

The right primer is applied to all bare surfaces and problem areas for maximum adhesion and coverage.

5

Paint

Premium paint applied in the correct coats for a smooth, even, durable finish on every surface.

6

Cleanup & Inspection

We clean everything up and do a final walkthrough with you to make sure every detail is perfect.

Quality Promise

We Don’t Cut Corners

The biggest difference between a good paint job and a great one is preparation. At Shawn Christie Painting, we spend more time prepping than most companies spend on the entire job — because we know that's what makes results last.

We use premium paints — not the cheap stuff — because the difference in longevity and appearance is night and day. Florida's sun will fade and degrade inferior paint quickly; our products are formulated to hold up in Florida's harsh climate.
