Vinyl Flooring

The Smart Choice for Florida Homes

Vinyl flooring has come a long way. Today's luxury vinyl plank (LVP) and vinyl tile look stunning, stand up to Florida's humidity, and are far more affordable than hardwood or stone. It's the perfect flooring solution for the Sarasota lifestyle.

Shawn Christie brings his construction expertise to every floor installation, ensuring proper subfloor preparation, accurate layout, and a finished result that looks flawless and lasts for decades.

  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) and sheet vinyl
  • 100% waterproof — ideal for Florida
  • Subfloor inspection and preparation
  • Precise measurement and layout
  • Clean, tight seams and transitions
  • Available in dozens of styles and colors
