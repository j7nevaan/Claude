How to Choose the Perfect Paint Colors for Your Florida Home

Choosing paint colors is one of the most exciting — and stressful — parts of any painting project. Florida's unique light makes color selection even more important. Here's how to choose colors that look great in Sarasota's sunshine.

Understand Florida's Light

Florida's intense sunlight washes out colors and makes them appear lighter than they look on paint chips. What looks like a soft sage green on a swatch can look almost white on your exterior in direct noon sun. Always go a shade darker than you think you want.

Popular Exterior Colors for Sarasota Homes

Warm whites, soft grays, coastal blues, and earthy tones all work beautifully with Florida's tropical landscaping and blue skies. Dark accent colors on doors and shutters add drama and contrast.

Test Before You Commit

Always test paint colors on your actual walls or exterior in a large swatch (at least 12"x12"). Look at it in morning light, afternoon light, and artificial light before making a final decision.

Interior Color Tips

Cool colors (blues, greens, grays) feel refreshing in Florida's heat. Light colors make small rooms feel larger. If your home gets a lot of natural light, you can go bolder with deeper colors without the room feeling dark.
