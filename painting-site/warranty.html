Our Promise

We Stand Behind Every Job

One of the things that sets Shawn Christie Painting & Pressure Washing apart is our commitment to standing behind our work. After more than 20 years in business, our reputation is everything — and we protect it by making sure every customer is genuinely satisfied.

Before we consider any job complete, we do a thorough walkthrough with you. We point out everything we've done, address any concerns, and make any touch-ups needed to ensure the finished result is exactly what you expected.

If something isn't right, we come back and make it right. It's that simple.
