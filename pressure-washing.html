Pressure Washing

Professional-Grade Cleaning Power

Florida's humidity creates the perfect environment for mold, mildew, algae, and grime to build up on exterior surfaces. Regular pressure washing isn't just about looks — it protects your home from biological growth that can damage paint, stucco, wood, and concrete over time.

With the right equipment and techniques, Shawn Christie removes years of buildup safely and effectively without damaging your surfaces.

  • Safe for stucco, wood, and concrete
  • Removes mold, algae, and mildew
  • Prepares surfaces for painting or sealing
  • Eco-friendly cleaning solutions used
  • Commercial-grade equipment
  • Soft-wash option for delicate surfaces
