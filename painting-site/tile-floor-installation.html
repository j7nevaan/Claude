Tile Installation

Precision Tile Work That Stands the Test of Time

Tile is one of the most durable and beautiful flooring options available — and it's perfectly suited to Florida's climate. From large-format porcelain to classic subway tiles and natural stone, Shawn Christie installs tile with the precision and craftsmanship that comes from over 20 years in the trade.

  • Porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone tile
  • Floor, wall, and shower tile installation
  • Proper subfloor preparation and leveling
  • Precision cutting for perfect fit
  • Expert grout application and sealing
  • Large-format and custom pattern layouts
