Our Story

Built on Quality, Rooted in Community

Shawn Christie started his company in 2004, bringing years of general construction experience to the painting and exterior services trade. What began as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted local business serving hundreds of homes and commercial properties across Sarasota and Bradenton.

Before launching his painting company, Shawn spent years in general construction, giving him a comprehensive understanding of how homes are built — and how to properly prepare and protect every surface he works on.

Shawn lives and works in the communities he serves. That local connection means he genuinely cares about the results and takes personal pride in every project, whether it’s a full interior repaint or a driveway pressure wash.

Today, Shawn Christie Painting & Pressure Washing Inc. offers six core services — from interior and exterior painting to paver restoration, pressure washing, and floor installation — all backed by over two decades of hands-on expertise.

Background

From Construction to Craftsmanship

Shawn’s background in general construction gave him a unique advantage: a deep understanding of building materials, substrates, and long-term durability. He knows how surfaces expand and contract in Florida’s climate, which paints and sealers hold up best, and how proper preparation makes all the difference between a job that lasts two years and one that lasts twenty.

That expertise, combined with two decades of real-world experience on thousands of Sarasota and Bradenton properties, is what sets Shawn Christie apart from the competition.
