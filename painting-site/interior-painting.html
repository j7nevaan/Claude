Interior Painting

Beautiful Interiors, Flawless Finishes

Your home's interior deserves more than a quick roll-and-run paint job. At Shawn Christie Painting, we take the time to properly prepare every surface — filling holes, sanding imperfections, masking trim — before applying premium paints that deliver a smooth, lasting finish.

Whether you're refreshing a single bedroom or repainting your entire home, we handle every room with the same care and professionalism that has made us Sarasota's trusted choice for over 20 years.

  • All furniture and floors protected
  • Proper surface prep and priming
  • Premium paints for lasting results
  • Clean, precise cut-in lines
  • Minimal disruption to your daily life
  • Full cleanup when we're done
