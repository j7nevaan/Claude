Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see yours here, call or text us at (941) 321-3573.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We offer completely free, no-obligation estimates for all of our services. We'll come to your property, assess the work, and provide a clear, detailed quote with no pressure.
How long has Shawn Christie been in business?
Shawn Christie Painting & Pressure Washing Inc. was founded in 2004. That's over 20 years of serving homeowners and businesses across Sarasota and Bradenton.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Sarasota and Bradenton, including Siesta Key, Lakewood Ranch, Venice, North Sarasota, South Sarasota, Longboat Key, and surrounding communities.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, Shawn Christie Painting & Pressure Washing Inc. is fully licensed and insured for your protection and peace of mind.
Can I text your phone number?
Absolutely! Our number (941) 321-3573 is fully textable. Send us a message anytime and we'll get back to you quickly.
What are your business hours?
We work Monday through Saturday from 6AM to 9PM. On Sundays, Shawn takes calls all day. We understand home improvement projects don't always happen on a schedule!
Do you paint both interior and exterior?
Yes! We handle both interior and exterior painting. We're experienced with all Florida home types including stucco, wood siding, block, and more.
How long does an exterior paint job take?
A typical single-family home exterior takes 2–4 days depending on the size, condition, and number of coats required. We'll give you a more specific timeline with your estimate.
How often should I have my pavers sealed?
In Florida, we generally recommend sealing pavers every 2–3 years, depending on sun exposure, foot traffic, and the type of sealer used. We'll advise you based on your specific situation.
Is pressure washing safe for my home?
Yes, when done correctly. We use the appropriate pressure for each surface and offer soft-wash options for delicate surfaces like roofs and painted wood. We never use high pressure where it could cause damage.
Do you do commercial work?
Yes, we serve both residential and commercial clients. If you have a commercial property that needs painting, pressure washing, or flooring, reach out and we'll put together a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and most major payment methods. Payment details will be outlined clearly in your estimate before any work begins.
