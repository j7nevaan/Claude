Our Floor Installation Process

From subfloor prep to the final trim piece, here's how we deliver a flawless floor installation.

How It Works

Floor Installation, Step by Step

1

Free Estimate

We measure the space, assess the subfloor, and provide a clear quote with material and labor.

2

Material Selection

We help you choose the right vinyl or tile product for your style, budget, and lifestyle.

3

Subfloor Prep

The existing floor is removed, subfloor inspected and leveled as needed for a perfect base.

4

Layout Planning

We plan the layout carefully to minimize cuts and ensure a professional, balanced appearance.

5

Installation

Flooring is installed row by row with precision cutting for doorways, obstacles, and transitions.

6

Trim & Cleanup

Baseboards, transitions, and trim are installed and the area is cleaned and ready to enjoy.
